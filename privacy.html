Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Brandy ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide when shopping on our website. By placing an order, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following information:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (optional, if provided)
  • Delivery address (area, street, building, floor, apartment)
  • Order details (product name, size, colour, quantity)

We also collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics, such as pages visited and time spent on the site. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and fulfil your order

We do not use your personal information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

We share your name, phone number, and delivery address with our delivery couriers solely for the purpose of completing your order. Couriers are not authorised to use this information for any other purpose.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors use the site (such as pages viewed and session duration). This information helps us improve the website experience and does not personally identify you.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use any advertising or retargeting cookies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your order information for as long as necessary to fulfil your order and comply with any applicable legal or accounting requirements. Once this period has passed, your personal data is securely deleted.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Payment confirmations sent via WhatsApp are used solely to verify your transaction and are not stored beyond what is necessary.

Please note that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
